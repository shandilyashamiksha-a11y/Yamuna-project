What is YARI?

YARI stands for Yamuna River Initiative, a student-led movement focused on protecting and restoring the Yamuna River. Its main goal is to spread awareness about river pollution, encourage clean-up drives, and promote eco-friendly habits among students and the community. The Yamuna River is one of India‚Äôs most important rivers and a major tributary of the Ganga River, but it faces serious pollution problems, especially in Delhi. Through activities like awareness campaigns, creative projects, and educational programs, YARI aims to inspire young people to take responsibility and work together to preserve this vital river for future generations.
